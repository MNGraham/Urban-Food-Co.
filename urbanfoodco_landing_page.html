Our Story

Texas Grown. Veteran Built.

Urban Food Company is a controlled-environment agriculture operation based in Manor, TX — 20 minutes from downtown Austin. We grow premium gourmet and medicinal mushrooms year-round using FarmBox Foods technology, producing consistent, chemical-free harvests regardless of season or weather.

Founded by a U.S. Army veteran and Texas A&M horticulture graduate, Urban Food Company brings military-grade operational discipline to every harvest. Every batch is logged, every delivery is traceable, and every product meets the standard your kitchen or storefront demands.

  • FarmBox Foods Gourmet Mushroom Farm — precision controlled environment
  • CannaCobra tissue culture lab — in-house spawn + micropropagation
  • Zero synthetic chemicals, pesticides, or herbicides
  • Full batch traceability from spore to harvest
  • Go Texan certified · Homegrown for Heroes eligible
  • Texas A&M AgriLife Extension consulting partnership

Why Urban Food Co.

What Sets Us Apart.

01
Year-Round Consistency

Our FarmBox controlled environment operates independently of Texas weather. No seasonal gaps, no drought failures, no pest pressure. Same quality, every week, every month.

02
Harvested to Order

We don't warehouse product. Every delivery is harvested within 24–48 hours of your order. That's the difference between a mushroom that's 2 days old and one that's 10 days old.

03
Full Traceability

Every batch carries a log number, harvest date, and production record. We can trace any product from your kitchen back to the specific grow cycle it came from.

04
Zero Chemicals

No synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or petroleum-based fertilizers. Ever. Our controlled environment eliminates the need — and we'll prove it with our grow records on request.

05
Hyper-Local

Manor, TX to your Austin kitchen in under an hour. No distributor middleman, no cold-chain degradation, no mystery about where your product came from. We're your neighbor.

06
Veteran Accountability

Military logistics, Texas A&M horticulture science, and an 8-year facility lease. We're not a hobby operation. We're built to deliver — on time, every time, to standard.
